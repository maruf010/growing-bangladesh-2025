Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovision supports government agencies, private sector, NGOs, and civil society with data, analytics, and strategy to design, manage and evaluate inclusive solutions.

Sustainability

Bangladesh has made rapid social and economic progress, but this has come at the cost of significant environmental damage. The World Bank and the Government of Bangladesh.

Infrastructure

South Asia’s "Land of Rivers" has long faced infrastructure challenges, but from tunnels and bridges to a new deep-sea port, engineering is transforming Bangladesh.

Empowerment

Bangladesh has made remarkable progress in the last 20 years in improving the lives of women and girls. The maternal mortality rate has decreased by more than two-thirds since 2000.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Green Energy

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
